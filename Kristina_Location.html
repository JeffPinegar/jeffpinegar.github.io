Average Data Science Salaries by Location


This page reviews average salaries by country. 607 reported salaries from 50 different countries are included in the data. The salary average for each country includes information from years 2020 through 2022 and has been converted to USD currency for the sake of global comparison. The highest number of reported salaries by country was the United States (355). The highest number of reported salaries by year was 2022 (318). The top 5 countries with the highest average reported salaries were Russia ($157,500), United States ($144,055), New Zealand ($125,00), Israel ($199,059), and Japan ($114,127). The bottom 5 countries with the lowest average reported salaries were Ukraine ($13,400), Pakistan ($13,333), Kenya ($9,272), Vietnam ($4000), and Iran ($4000).
